Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I delete photos? Swipe left on any photo to delete it from your library.
  • How do I keep photos? Swipe right on any photo to keep it in your library.
  • How can I track my progress? Check the progress bar in the app’s dashboard to see how many photos you’ve reviewed.
  • What should I do if the app crashes? Please restart the app and ensure it’s updated to the latest version. If the issue persists, contact support.
  • How can I provide feedback? We welcome your feedback! Please email us at feedback@picswipeapp.com.

Technical Support

For technical issues, please provide the following details to help us resolve your problem quickly:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 13, iPad Air)
  • Operating System version (e.g., iOS 16.1)
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Any error messages you’ve encountered

Send this information to techsupport@picswipeapp.com.
